A little about me

I am enough of an artist to draw freely upon my imagination. The point is that when I see a sunset or a waterfall or

something, for a split second it's so great, because for a little bit I'm out of my brain, and it's got nothing to do with

me. I'm not trying to figure it out, you know what I mean? And I wonder if I can somehow find a way to maintain that

mind stillness.

I like things with colors moving from one place to another, creative backgrounds etc.
